Introduction to GRACE Center
Shinichi Honiden (National Institute of Informatics)

Abstract:

GRACE Center is a world-leading software research center in National
Institute of Informatics engaged in research, education and practical
work in alliances with research organizations in Japan and overseas
and as part of industry-academia collaboration. GRACE Center seeks to
put in place the foundations of 21st century software, while
developing world-class researchers and engineers who will go on to
play central roles in the next generation.
